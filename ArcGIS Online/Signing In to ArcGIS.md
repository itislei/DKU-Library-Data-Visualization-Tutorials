# Signing In to ArcGIS

This guide provides instructions for signing in ArcGIS Online using your Duke NetID. ArcGIS Online is a web-based mapping system compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux systems. [file:1]

## Table of Contents

1. Signing in using NetID  
2. Learning Resources  
3. Troubleshooting  

## Signing in using NetID

1. Open ArcGIS Online (https://www.arcgis.com/index.html) in your browser and click Sign In, located either in the center or in the top right corner. [file:1]  
2. Select Your ArcGIS organization’s URL. [file:1]  
3. Type in `Dukeuniv` in the box and click Continue. [file:1]  
4. Click Duke University. [file:1]  
5. Log in through the Duke login page using your NetID and password. [file:1]  

You’re now signed in to ArcGIS Online! [file:1]
