# Downloading, Installing and Licensing ArcGIS Pro

This guide provides instructions for downloading, installing, accessing and licensing ArcGIS Pro on a Windows computer using your Duke NetID. ArcGIS Pro is Esri’s professional desktop GIS software for advanced mapping, spatial analysis, and data visualization.

Please note that ArcGIS Pro is available only for Windows currently. If you are using a Mac, check the solutions from [here](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://pro.arcgis.com/en/pro-app/latest/get-started/run-pro-on-a-mac.htm&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1770261833995401&usg=AOvVaw3-vDWL4T9BApMJ5i3rvJlE).

Signing in ArcGIS
=================

Follow the instructions in [Signing in to ArcGIS](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://drive.google.com/file/d/1SyECd73MedrKOyBwMv14BXTZ0CHXBxBc/view?usp%3Ddrive_link&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1770261833996722&usg=AOvVaw1nLh3ifdCecODcn7muQUqM).

Downloading ArcGIS Pro​
=======================

1.  On the ArcGIS Online page, click your profile icon in the top right corner, then select My settings from the dropdown menu.

<img width="824" height="790" alt="image17" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/76fa63a9-2af8-4414-8098-01ecbe93d1b1" />


2.  Navigate to the Licenses tab from the side menu.

<img width="640" height="596" alt="image4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/30278628-9cdd-4e96-9211-555b392ddc21" />


3.  Locate ArcGIS Pro in the list, and click on Download ArcGIS Pro.

<img width="1610" height="588" alt="image3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9172d355-752a-4eba-b75b-599eec2fc02e" />


4.  Select your preferred language and click Download.

<img width="1548" height="990" alt="image5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/19b4ecde-844c-43f4-8471-87d99f40c1f3" />


Installing ArcGIS Pro
=====================

1.  After the download is complete, locate the ArcGIS Pro installer in your Downloads and open it.
2.  Choose a destination folder on your computer and click Next.

<img width="696" height="540" alt="image6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cf63ed80-2601-49e8-a1ee-a148f03f00e9" />


3.  Click Close.

<img width="696" height="540" alt="image2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3463eabe-ddd7-42b9-b268-01b3309b1239" />


4.  Click Next.

<img width="630" height="481" alt="image13" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7ac61331-4da2-4bc9-b814-90f80514dca1" />


5.  Click Next.

<img width="631" height="482" alt="image11" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ddc036c8-6233-4221-8387-c3927e0814f6" />


6.  Check ‘I accept the master agreement’ and click Next.

<img width="632" height="481" alt="image8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e441db77-b836-4a88-9e12-96a676dc3ab6" />


7.  Select access type and click Next.

<img width="633" height="483" alt="image16" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9ada2cc9-54b6-4219-9412-b9c1c7591c4b" />


8.  You can choose to install additional features. Let’s keep it as default now and click Next to continue.

<img width="633" height="485" alt="image10" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/77edf39d-878d-4b61-89a8-00075b7ea70f" />


9.  Click Install.

<img width="632" height="484" alt="image1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1de2b8bd-9c12-4bb3-9147-c398c16b9db9" />


10.  Click Finish.

<img width="630" height="480" alt="image9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5eb24070-2161-4d4d-80e0-0bc7b1b34812" />


Accessing and Licensing using NetID​
====================================

1.  Open ArcGIS Pro on your computer.
2.  Select Your ArcGIS organization’s URL.

<img width="879" height="692" alt="image7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b428b5e8-e3bf-41d5-905d-caa4248a6e0f" />


3.  Type in ‘Dukeuniv’ in the box and click Continue.

<img width="879" height="691" alt="image12" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7c81061e-f26f-4132-be71-2f3ffc5f109c" />


4.  Click Duke University.

<img width="885" height="692" alt="image14" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cf4ea395-c6ce-4e24-9dce-b4d823d9c5a7" />


5.  Log in through the Duke login page using your NetID and password.

<img width="880" height="696" alt="image15" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/760baa56-a5af-4994-ad76-a48c47ea7d23" />


6.  You’re now logged in to ArcGIS Pro!

Troubleshooting
===============

If you encounter any technical issues installing or using ArcGIS Online or ArcGIS Pro, [Esri Community](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://community.esri.com/&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1770261834004233&usg=AOvVaw1gpb2i6rIalFLYpgpWDjd5) is a good place to start.

If the problem persists or your issue is not addressed there, please contact the Data and Visualization Librarian, Siti Lei ([siti.lei@dukekunshan.edu.cn](mailto:siti.lei@dukekunshan.edu.cn)) for further support.
